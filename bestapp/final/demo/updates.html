BHARAT EXCELLENCE SCHOLARSHIP TEST

Students from 6th to 10th class can only apply for Bharat Excellence Scholarship Test (BEST). Registrations will start soon. For more updates stay tuned to BEST. Contact No. +91 95043 23456, +91 91693 23456

::Latest Updates::

  • Registration Fee is Rs.500/-
  • Test will be held on 15th December of 2019.
  • Test will be online mode only.
  • There will be two mock tests before the Test.
  • Students can take test either through Application or Web.
  • Registrations will be closed on 10th December of 2019

Who Can Apply

Students from 6th to 10th Class throughout INDIA can apply.

Syllabus

Syllabus is based on your subjects English, Maths and Science(Physics and Biology).

And also Logical Reasoning and General Knowledge
