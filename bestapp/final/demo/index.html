Marks

Logical Reasoning 20 Marks

Total 120 Marks

**Time Duration ---- 1 hour 30 mins (90minutes)

**Each Question Carry 1 Mark

**No Negative Marking

INSTRUCTIONS FOR TEST

1. Make sure you have a good INTERNET connection.

2. Test LOGIN details will be sent to your mail id and make sure to note them.

3. Candidates should BEGIN the exam sharply in alloted time.

4. Candidates should NOT keep aside books and take help from others

5. Ensure that NO APPLICATION is open other than TEST application. If you are using browser , dont minimize it. If you minimize the browser it may result in automatic cancellation of Exam.

6. After 60 MINUTES, automatically your answers will be submitted.
