About BEST

BHARAT EXCELLENCE SCHOLARSHIP TEST here in referred as BEST which is an online scholarship test, conducted by Avira Foundation in order to bring out hidden talent in students. BEST honours and supports the talented students across India which will be the way for their higher education. Main objective of the scholarship test is to identify students with potential to study and support them with cash rewards.Students from 6th to 10th class with any board can apply for the BEST.

Our Mission

Promote and provide financial support for students education and welfare we inspire and enable a better growth of student's through our scholarship programme.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant and supportive community of scholars by markedly expanding opportunities to connect and explore Ideas.

Instructions For Test

1. Make sure you have a good INTERNET connection.

2. Test LOGIN details will be sent to your mail id and make sure to note them.

3. Candidates should BEGIN the exam sharply at alloted time.

4. Candidates should NOT keep aside books and take help from others

5. Ensure that NO APPLICATION is open other than TEST application. If you are using browser , dont minimize it. If you minimize the browser it may result in auto cancellation of Exam.

6. After 90 MINUTES, automatically your answers will be submitted.
